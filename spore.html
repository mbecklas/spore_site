    • So, what is spore?

    Spore is a game where you creat a species and decide the path that this specie will follow, for example you can creat a herbivorous animal and trought our actions in the world creat a peacefull nation. Or you can be a also a herbevorous species, but trought your decisons became a interplanetery dictator. You chose your fate and destiny, the main go of the game is to reach the center of the galaxy, but you can do what you want to reach it.


    When we talk about spore's gameplay, it is really important to mention that the game has five stages:

    1. Celular
    2. Creature
    3. Tribal
    4. City
    5. Spacial

    The celular stage

    This is the first stage from the game, you arrive from a metiore and now you are part of this planet ecosystem, as a tiny cell. Here you will need to eat so earn DNA points and grow to evolve. In the beggining of the game you can chose to be a vegetarian species or a carnivorous species, if you are a carnivorouos you need to use your creature to kill others and eat their meat. If you are a vegetarian you need to eat plants and survive predadors attacks. You can use the DNA points you earned by eating when you mate with another one of your species, you do this by clicking a button. After you mate a egg is born, and you can edit the creature inside this egg to buy upgrades for example spikes or better locomotion, this will cost you DNA points, so I'ts always good saving it. After you grow enough you can get your legs in the next matting and evolve to the next stage!

    The creature stage

    Now you have a leg and a tribe, but you are not alone in this world. Insted of simply eating to evolve you have two opitions, you can eighter became friend with other tibres trought posing, singing and dancing, or you can murder all their male alphas and extermine the babys trought punhces, screthces, charges and poison to win DNA points. The more tribres you kill/becomes friend the bigger your brain becomes, you can still mate to spend DNA in usefull upgrades, but after some time of evolving you are able to call friends from your tribe to help you in your missions. The more you evolve the more members you can call. After becoming big brain enought you and your mates descover fire and decide to create a primitive tribe, wich is our next stage!

    The tribal stage

    In this stage you will start the first steps to build a civilization! Here you will use food as a resource, if your animal was herbivorous you can colect food or tame a creature and colect it eggs for food. If you were a carnivorous you can colect food eighter by fishing, killing less evolved animals or taming them. In this stage you will evolve by allying or disimating the other tribes, you can ally with them by singing musics, with diferent instruments and trained musitions. You can attack them using different 3 types of weapons and healers, and create your own strategies to have a beter position at combat. After you are done with the other tribres you will evolve and creat a ctitie, where your next stage will start!

    The city stage

    In this stage you will not control creatures, you will control tanks, airplanes and boats. You will mainly edit your cities, so they become the most lucrative and happy as possible. For winning this stage you must conquer all the other cities. for doing this you have 3 opitions, the first one is trought religion. This cars will go into other cities and orquerstrate attacks to spread the world of your God and the citie owners will try to stop this by breaking your cars with guns. The second opition is trought direct force, you will attack and crush your enemies using diverse military strategies. And the third one is trought money, you can by other cities after creating comertial rotes with them and paying a large amount of money. Money is really important in this stage, so you can build your cities with 3 types of construction, you can built a house, to increase the populatuion, built a factory to increase money earned and use theaters to increase the happynes and prevent religious take overs to be so easy.

    The space stage

    The last and longuer stage! After conquering the other cities it's time for your greatest chalenge. Reaching the center of the galaxy and spreading your empire trought the galaxy. But, for doing this stuffy you need money, and for having money you can do mitions to other galatic empires, that will give money and increase your friend ship with them. Buy spices in empires that sell them at a low cost and sell it to empires that pay high for it. Or you can spread a toon of colonies trought the galaxy and go there take spices for free and sell it to other empires. In this stage it is important to spread your empire as most as possible, so you can make aliences and wars with other empires to spred your influces. When you win a war or ally to an empire your ship is able to recive more parts and upgrades. with this you will be able to truly end the game by reaching the center of the galaxy and discovering the truth about the universe and reciving God tools to play arround the galaxy!

    Why is the evolution part wrong?

    So, I really love this game, but the evolution idea is passed in a completly wrong way, and the problem is that the game sells it self as an evolution game. In the game one baby is born with a toon of different modifications, that are not developed by the enviroment, but by a inteligent degsiner. In real life small changes take a lot of time to occor, and they are made by the enviroment and the survival of the fittest. In the game this does not happen, and it is one inacurace.
