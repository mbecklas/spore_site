Spore

The best not acurate game about evolution.

Spore is a game where you creat a species and decide the path that this specie will follow, for example you can creat a herbivorous animal and trought our actions in the world creat a peacefull nation. Or you can be a also a herbevorous species, but trought your decisons became a interplanetery dictator. You chose your fate and destiny, the main go of the game is to reach the center of the galaxy, but you can do what you want to reach it.
